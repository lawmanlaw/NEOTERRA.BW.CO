01 · ABOUT NEOTERRA

Bridging the gap between complex spatial data and actionable insight

Neoterra GIS Services is a dynamic, forward-thinking geospatial company founded by a team of passionate geospatial professionals. We believe accurate geographic information is the foundation of sound decision-making, for government planning, environmental management, infrastructure development, and private sector investment.

VISION

The leading GIS services provider in Southern Africa, recognized for innovation, precision, and impact.

MISSION

To empower organizations and communities through cutting-edge geospatial technology and expert spatial analysis, enabling smarter planning, better resource management, and sustainable development.

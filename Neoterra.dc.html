01 — ABOUT NEOTERRA

Bridging the gap between complex spatial data and actionable insight

Neoterra GIS Services is a dynamic, forward-thinking geospatial company delivering high-quality Geographic Information System solutions across Botswana and the broader region. Founded by a team of passionate geospatial professionals, we believe accurate geographic information is the foundation of sound decision-making — for government planning, environmental management, infrastructure development, and private sector investment.

OUR VISION

To be the leading GIS services provider in Southern Africa, recognized for innovation, precision, and impact.

OUR MISSION

To empower organizations and communities through cutting-edge geospatial technology and expert spatial analysis — enabling smarter planning, better resource management, and sustainable development.

02 — OUR SERVICES

A comprehensive suite of GIS services

Tailored to the needs of government agencies, private companies, NGOs, and development organizations.

GIS Mapping & Cartography

Accurate, high-quality maps for planning, reporting, and decision-making.

Spatial Data Analysis

Advanced analysis of geographic datasets to uncover patterns and support strategic planning.

Remote Sensing & Imagery

Interpretation of satellite and aerial imagery for land use, vegetation, and terrain analysis.

GIS Database Management

Field Data Collection

GIS Consulting & Training

Expert advisory services and hands-on training for organizations adopting GIS technology.
